Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We’re here to help! Check out our FAQs below for quick answers.

What are the delivery options?

We offer both pickup and delivery options. Delivery charges vary based on location and will be calculated at checkout.

Can I customize my cream puff orders?

Yes! We offer customization for large orders. Please contact us directly to discuss your preferences.

What is your return and refund policy?

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns. However, if you encounter an issue, please contact us within 24 hours.

Are your products halal-certified?

Yes, all our products are made using halal-certified ingredients.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order status via your account dashboard under the 'Orders' section.
